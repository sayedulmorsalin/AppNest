Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At AppNest, accessible from https://appnests.vercel.app, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by AppNest and how we use it.

We may collect information in two ways: information you provide (like contact details) and information automatically collected (usage data, cookies).

2. Google AdSense and Cookies

AppNest uses third-party vendor Google to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to AppNest and/or other sites on the Internet.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on AppNest.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Log Files

AppNest follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

4. Third-Party Privacy Policies

AppNest's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our contact page.
